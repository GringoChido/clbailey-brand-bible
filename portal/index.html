Module 01

Brand Foundation — The Strategic Anchor

We do not compete on the showroom floor. We compete on the timeline of a home.

The Three Pillars

I · Craftsmanship as Proof
Solid hardwood, 1-inch diamond-honed slate, 8 hand-applied finishes. A spec sheet, not an adjective.
II · Structural Endurance
Built for residential longevity measured in decades. Re-levelable. Handed down, not hauled off.
III · Multi-Functional Space
Game night to dinner party. Convertible dining tops and matched-family collections.

The Three Personas

The Discerning Homeowner
"Will this look like a bar, or like furniture?" Win with: Furniture first. Game table second.
The Legacy Family
"Is this a purchase or an investment?" Win with: Buy it once. Pass it down.
The Dealer Principal
"Will this protect my margin and not blow up in a claim?" Win with: Sell it with confidence.
Positioning statement. C.L. Bailey & Co. sits at the intersection of premium residential furniture design and championship-level play performance — solid hardwood on 1-inch diamond-honed slate, finished by hand in 8 coats, engineered to be inherited. We are the table that is still in the room a generation later.

Module 02 · Live

Brand Color Architecture

Drawn from real materials: billiard felt, cast iron, three signature woods, honed slate, cast brass. Deep Heritage Emerald is the governing anchor. Click any swatch to copy its HEX. Proportion law: Emerald 60 / Iron + Wood 30 / Slate + Brass 10. Brass never exceeds 5%.
