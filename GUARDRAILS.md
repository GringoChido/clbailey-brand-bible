# C.L. BAILEY & CO. — BRAND GUARDRAILS
### Bulletproof, copy-paste rules for the Process

> These guardrails are the enforceable distillation of the full Brand Bible (`spec/BRAND_BIBLE.md`). They are written to be pasted at the top of any session, brief, or asset review. If any output violates a **MUST** below, it is rejected — no dealer or creator discretion.

---

## 0. THE STANDING GUARDRAIL PROMPT (paste at session start)

> You are working on a C.L. Bailey & Co. brand asset. C.L. Bailey & Co. (est. 1999, Tomball, TX) makes handcrafted solid-hardwood game room furniture — tagline "Built for Homes That Endure." The palette mirrors clbailey.com: Warm White (#FAF8F5) grounds, Graphite (#3A3A3A) ink, Deep Charcoal (#1C1C1E) structure, Aged Bronze (#A89279) and Sand (#D4C9B8) accents; Heritage Green (#006840) appears ONLY in the logo lettering and the felt. Headlines are Le Jour Serif (400, never bolded); body is Roboto; labels and buttons are Raleway 11px uppercase with +2px tracking. Before producing anything, load these guardrails and self-check the output against the MUST / NEVER lists. Solid hardwood only — never MDF. 1-inch diamond-honed slate. 8 hand-applied finishes. Sold through Authorized Dealers; CTA is always "Find a Dealer." Voice: master carpenter explaining their work — premium not elitist, traditional not antiquated, durable not industrial. Oxford comma always; max one exclamation point. If a claim isn't in the approved facts, ask or omit — never invent.

## 1. MATERIAL & PRODUCT TRUTH (never compromise)

| ✅ MUST | ⛔ NEVER |
|---|---|
| Say "solid hardwood only" | Imply or hide MDF / particle board / veneer substrate |
| State the numbers: 1-inch diamond-honed slate, 8 hand-applied finishes | Round-trick or soften specs |
| Lead with "game room furniture" | Lead with "pool table" as the primary noun |
| Use the sanctioned "No MDF" contrast claim verbatim | Exaggerate the contrast claim or apply it to unverified competitors |
| Reference collections by proper name (Skylar, Viking, Tunbridge, Dutchess — never translated) | Invent collections, finishes, dimensions, or guarantees |

**The one sanctioned use of "MDF":**
> "Most tables in this price range hide MDF or particle board under a veneer. We don't. Solid hardwood, all the way through — because an heirloom can't be hollow."

---

## 2. VISUAL GUARDRAILS

- **MUST** ground every layout on Warm White `#FAF8F5` (or invert to Deep Charcoal `#1C1C1E` for full-bleed dark sections). Proportion: **Warm White/Cloud 60 / Charcoal + Photography 30 / Sand + Bronze 10**. Bronze **MUST NOT** exceed 5% of any surface.
- **MUST** keep logo clear space ≥ `1×C` (digital) / `1.5×C` (print), where C = cap height of the leading "C".
- **MUST** meet minimum reproduction sizes: Primary 180px / 1.25in; Lockup 120px / 0.875in; Crest legible at 16px single-color.
- **NEVER** recolor, stretch, outline, or rebuild a mark. **NEVER** set Bronze, Sand, or a wood tone as small body text on a light ground. **NEVER** use Heritage Green outside the unaltered logo and the felt.
- **Color tokens (exact):** Graphite `#3A3A3A` · Deep Charcoal `#1C1C1E` · True Black `#141413` · Mid Gray `#6B6560` · Bronze `#A89279` · Sand `#D4C9B8` · Cloud `#EDE8E0` · Warm White `#FAF8F5` · Heritage Green `#006840` (mark & felt only).
- **Type (exact):** Le Jour Serif 400 for all headlines (never bolded, never below 19px); Roboto 400 body at 16px in Mid Gray; Raleway 500/600 uppercase labels and buttons (11px, +2px tracking). Buttons: 2px radius, 1px border, 12×28px padding. No drop shadows anywhere.
- **Motion (§2.8):** micro 200–300ms `ease`; entrances 400–600ms `ease-out`, translate ≤24px, stagger 100–150ms. **NEVER** parallax, scroll-jacking, bounce easing, hover scale >1.02, carousels <6s, or an animated logo. `prefers-reduced-motion` MUST be honored.
- **Marks reality check:** only the Primary badge exists in production. Lockup and Crest are specified, not built (§2.1.4) — **NEVER** improvise either; interim rule is the badge on its Warm White plate.
- **File naming MUST follow:** `[Brand]-[Module]-[AssetType]-[Color]-[Version].[Ext]` → e.g. `CLB-LOGO-PrimaryMark-Full-v01.svg`. Unnamed files are purged on monthly audit.

## 3. VOICE & COPY GUARDRAILS

- **MUST:** Oxford comma always. Maximum **one (1)** exclamation point per piece; **zero** in spec/warranty copy. Spell one–nine; figures for 10+ and all measurements/prices. Capitalize collection names.
- **Positioning lines (memorize):** premium not elitist · traditional not antiquated · durable not industrial · confident not arrogant · warm not folksy.
- **Social structure MUST be:** Hook (1 line, no emoji) → Core Narrative (2–4 lines) → Find-a-Dealer CTA. Max 2 approved emoji (🎱 🪵 🏡 ✨), never in the hook. 6–10 hashtags, always leading with `#CLBaileyCo #BuiltToEndure`.
- **BANNED WORDS (never):** MDF/particle board (except sanctioned claim), veneer substrate, cheap, budget, affordable, "man cave," "bar," "game room toys," "unit," disposable.
- **Spanish (Module 3-B):** **usted** always, never tú. Locked translations only — tagline "Construidas para Hogares que Perduran," CTA "Encontrar Distribuidor," "Solo Madera Maciza," "Garantía Estructural de por Vida." Collection names never translated. Accents mandatory, including on capitals. Max one ¡…! pair; zero in spec/warranty. **BANNED (ES):** barato, económico, asequible, cueva de hombre, bar, unidad, desechable. No machine translation ships unreviewed.

## 4. PRICING & LEGAL GUARDRAILS (paid + co-op)

- **MUST** label any price as **MSRP**. **NEVER** advertise below-MSRP figures or strikethrough "was/now" pricing in brand or co-op (brand-funded) creative.
- **CTA MUST be "Find a Dealer,"** never "Shop Now."
- **MUST** place the Lifetime Structural Guarantee Brass seal bottom-right (≥0.5in, never cropped) with its ® mark and a terms footnote.
- **Required legal micro-line (≥11px/6pt):** "C.L. Bailey & Co. products sold through Authorized Dealers. MSRP shown; dealer pricing may vary. Lifetime Structural Guarantee subject to terms at clbailey.com/guarantee."

---

## 5. ACCESSIBILITY GUARDRAILS (web / portal)

- **MUST** hit WCAG **AA** on every pair (4.5:1 normal text; 3:1 large ≥24px); prefer AAA pairs for long-form. Verified: Graphite-on-Warm-White 10.7:1 (AAA), Mid-Gray-on-Warm-White 5.4:1 (AA body standard), White-on-Deep-Charcoal 17.0:1 (AAA), Deep-Charcoal-on-Sand 10.4:1 (AAA), Bronze-on-Deep-Charcoal 5.7:1 (AA).
- **NEVER** use Aged Bronze `#A89279` or Sand `#D4C9B8` as text on a light ground (Bronze fails AA at 2.8:1). Accent/border/dark-ground use only.
- **Alt-text MUST follow:** `[Collection] [piece] in [finish] finish — C.L. Bailey & Co., [setting]`. Decorative images get `alt=""`. Missing product alt fails the monthly portal audit.
- **Machine readability (Module 11):** entity facts byte-identical everywhere (footer, JSON-LD, GBP, directories); Organization + Product JSON-LD required; `llms.txt` maintained; every spec claim exists as crawlable HTML text; hreflang `en`/`es` pairs on every page; Spanish pages carry `inLanguage: "es"`.

---

## 6. AI GUARDRAILS

- **MUST** prepend the Module 8 system prompt to any AI copy task.
- **AI-cliché ban list (never):** delve, testament, revolutionize, seamless/seamlessly, elevate, unlock, game-changer, tapestry, bustling, "in today's fast-paced world," "look no further," nestled, "whether you're… or…," "it's worth noting," embark, navigate (figurative), robust, synergy, boasts.
- **Image generation MUST** specify real dimensions, natural wood grain, matte hand-applied finish, warm residential lighting, and the negative prompt `--no plastic, glossy, warped perspective, melting wood, extra legs, floating table, white seamless studio background, CGI sheen`. **NEVER** pass AI imagery off as a real product photo in dealer-facing or paid assets.

---

## 7. CO-BRANDING GUARDRAILS

- **MUST** keep co-brand lockups at parity: C.L. Bailey mark between **85%–115%** of the retailer mark's optical height.
- **MUST** separate marks with a Sand divider (`#D4C9B8`) OR ≥ `2×C` gap; align on a shared baseline (horizontal) or center axis (stacked).
- **NEVER** let a retailer imply manufacture, partnership, or exclusivity without written brand-team approval. **MUST** retain the ®/™ and the attribution micro-line.

---

## 8. THE 60-SECOND REVIEW CHECKLIST (run before any asset ships)

1. Solid hardwood / spec numbers stated correctly? No banned words?
2. Warm-White-grounded, 60/30/10, bronze ≤5%? Heritage Green only in mark and felt?
3. Logo clear space + min size respected, mark unaltered?
4. Oxford comma; ≤1 exclamation point?
5. CTA = "Find a Dealer"? MSRP labeled; no strikethrough?
6. WCAG AAA contrast on all text? Alt-text in pattern?
7. File named to `[Brand]-[Module]-[AssetType]-[Color]-[Version].[Ext]`?
8. Co-brand parity + divider + attribution (if applicable)?
9. AI cliché ban-list clean (if AI-assisted)?
10. Spanish surface? Usted register, locked 3-B translations, accents on capitals, native review done?
11. Motion within §2.8 tokens; `prefers-reduced-motion` honored; logo static?
12. Final gut check: *would this still look right in this home in 2055?*
