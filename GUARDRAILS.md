# C.L. BAILEY & CO. — BRAND GUARDRAILS
### Bulletproof, copy-paste rules for the Process

> These guardrails are the enforceable distillation of the full Brand Bible (`spec/BRAND_BIBLE.md`). They are written to be pasted at the top of any session, brief, or asset review. If any output violates a **MUST** below, it is rejected — no dealer or creator discretion.

---

## 0. THE STANDING GUARDRAIL PROMPT (paste at session start)

> You are working on a C.L. Bailey & Co. brand asset. C.L. Bailey & Co. (est. 1999, Tomball, TX) makes handcrafted solid-hardwood game room furniture — tagline "Built for Homes That Endure." The anchor color is Deep Heritage Emerald (#14352A). Before producing anything, load these guardrails and self-check the output against the MUST / NEVER lists. Solid hardwood only — never MDF. 1-inch diamond-honed slate. 8 hand-applied finishes. Sold through Authorized Dealers; CTA is always "Find a Dealer." Voice: master carpenter explaining their work — premium not elitist, traditional not antiquated, durable not industrial. Oxford comma always; max one exclamation point. If a claim isn't in the approved facts, ask or omit — never invent.

---

## 1. MATERIAL & PRODUCT TRUTH (never compromise)

| ✅ MUST | ⛔ NEVER |
|---|---|
| Say "solid hardwood only" | Imply or hide MDF / particle board / veneer substrate |
| State the numbers: 1-inch diamond-honed slate, 8 hand-applied finishes | Round-trick or soften specs |
| Lead with "game room furniture" | Lead with "pool table" as the primary noun |
| Use the sanctioned "No MDF" contrast claim verbatim | Exaggerate the contrast claim or apply it to unverified competitors |
| Reference collections by proper name (Skylar, Viking, Tunbridge) | Invent collections, finishes, dimensions, or guarantees |

**The one sanctioned use of "MDF":**
> "Most tables in this price range hide MDF or particle board under a veneer. We don't. Solid hardwood, all the way through — because an heirloom can't be hollow."

---

## 2. VISUAL GUARDRAILS

- **MUST** anchor every layout on Deep Heritage Emerald `#14352A`. Proportion: **Emerald 60 / Iron + Wood 30 / Slate + Brass 10**. Brass **MUST NOT** exceed 5% of any surface.
- **MUST** keep logo clear space ≥ `1×C` (digital) / `1.5×C` (print), where C = cap height of the leading "C".
- **MUST** meet minimum reproduction sizes: Primary 180px / 1.25in; Lockup 120px / 0.875in; Crest legible at 16px single-color.
- **NEVER** recolor, stretch, outline, or rebuild a mark. **NEVER** place a wood tone or Slate Silver as small body text.
- **Color tokens (exact):** Emerald `#14352A` · Iron `#2B2B28` · Espresso `#3A2A1E` · Chestnut `#6B4423` · Mahogany `#7A2E1D` · Slate `#B8BCC0` · Brass `#B08D57` · Bone `#F4F1EA`.
- **File naming MUST follow:** `[Brand]-[Module]-[AssetType]-[Color]-[Version].[Ext]` → e.g. `CLB-LOGO-PrimaryMark-Emerald-v01.svg`. Unnamed files are purged on monthly audit.

---

## 3. VOICE & COPY GUARDRAILS

- **MUST:** Oxford comma always. Maximum **one (1)** exclamation point per piece; **zero** in spec/warranty copy. Spell one–nine; figures for 10+ and all measurements/prices. Capitalize collection names.
- **Positioning lines (memorize):** premium not elitist · traditional not antiquated · durable not industrial · confident not arrogant · warm not folksy.
- **Social structure MUST be:** Hook (1 line, no emoji) → Core Narrative (2–4 lines) → Find-a-Dealer CTA. Max 2 approved emoji (🎱 🪵 🏡 ✨), never in the hook. 6–10 hashtags, always leading with `#CLBaileyCo #BuiltToEndure`.
- **BANNED WORDS (never):** MDF/particle board (except sanctioned claim), veneer substrate, cheap, budget, affordable, "man cave," "bar," "game room toys," "unit," disposable.

---

## 4. PRICING & LEGAL GUARDRAILS (paid + co-op)

- **MUST** label any price as **MSRP**. **NEVER** advertise below-MSRP figures or strikethrough "was/now" pricing in brand or co-op (brand-funded) creative.
- **CTA MUST be "Find a Dealer,"** never "Shop Now."
- **MUST** place the Lifetime Structural Guarantee Brass seal bottom-right (≥0.5in, never cropped) with its ® mark and a terms footnote.
- **Required legal micro-line (≥11px/6pt):** "C.L. Bailey & Co. products sold through Authorized Dealers. MSRP shown; dealer pricing may vary. Lifetime Structural Guarantee subject to terms at clbailey.com/guarantee."

---

## 5. ACCESSIBILITY GUARDRAILS (web / portal)

- **MUST** hit WCAG **AAA** (7:1 normal text; 4.5:1 large ≥24px). Verified pairings: Iron-on-Bone 12.6:1, Bone-on-Emerald 11.8:1, Bone-on-Espresso 12.2:1, Bone-on-Mahogany 8.3:1.
- **NEVER** use Slate Silver `#B8BCC0` for small text on a light ground (fails AAA). It is an accent/border/UI color only.
- **Alt-text MUST follow:** `[Collection] [piece] in [finish] finish — C.L. Bailey & Co., [setting]`. Decorative images get `alt=""`. Missing product alt fails the monthly portal audit.

---

## 6. AI GUARDRAILS

- **MUST** prepend the Module 8 system prompt to any AI copy task.
- **AI-cliché ban list (never):** delve, testament, revolutionize, seamless/seamlessly, elevate, unlock, game-changer, tapestry, bustling, "in today's fast-paced world," "look no further," nestled, "whether you're… or…," "it's worth noting," embark, navigate (figurative), robust, synergy, boasts.
- **Image generation MUST** specify real dimensions, natural wood grain, matte hand-applied finish, warm residential lighting, and the negative prompt `--no plastic, glossy, warped perspective, melting wood, extra legs, floating table, white seamless studio background, CGI sheen`. **NEVER** pass AI imagery off as a real product photo in dealer-facing or paid assets.

---

## 7. CO-BRANDING GUARDRAILS

- **MUST** keep co-brand lockups at parity: C.L. Bailey mark between **85%–115%** of the retailer mark's optical height.
- **MUST** separate marks with a Slate Silver divider OR ≥ `2×C` gap; align on a shared baseline (horizontal) or center axis (stacked).
- **NEVER** let a retailer imply manufacture, partnership, or exclusivity without written brand-team approval. **MUST** retain the ®/™ and the attribution micro-line.

---

## 8. THE 60-SECOND REVIEW CHECKLIST (run before any asset ships)

1. Solid hardwood / spec numbers stated correctly? No banned words?
2. Emerald-anchored, 60/30/10, brass ≤5%?
3. Logo clear space + min size respected, mark unaltered?
4. Oxford comma; ≤1 exclamation point?
5. CTA = "Find a Dealer"? MSRP labeled; no strikethrough?
6. WCAG AAA contrast on all text? Alt-text in pattern?
7. File named to `[Brand]-[Module]-[AssetType]-[Color]-[Version].[Ext]`?
8. Co-brand parity + divider + attribution (if applicable)?
9. AI cliché ban-list clean (if AI-assisted)?
10. Final gut check: *would this still look right in this home in 2055?*
